Available animations are (names are somewhat self explaining):

  • slide up slideup
  • slide right slideright
  • slide down slidedown
  • slide left slideleft
  • fade up fadeup
  • fade right faderight
  • fade down fadedown
  • fade left fadeleft
  • fade fade
